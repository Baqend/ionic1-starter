Welcome to Ionic-Baqend-Starter

This is Baqends Ionic starter, which is based on the Ionic tabs starter.

To connect your ionic app to your Baqend instance change the variable appName in the service.js to the name of your Baqend instance. If you will use your app on iOS please replace app-starter with your Baqend instance name at the bottom ot the config.xml.

To edit the content of each tab, edit the corresponding template file in www/templates/. This template is www/templates/tab-dash.html

If you need help with Baqend feel free to join our forum or contact us via our website.
